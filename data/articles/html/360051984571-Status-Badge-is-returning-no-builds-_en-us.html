If your status badge is returning `no builds` even though you have recent `passing` or `failed` builds, this may be due to a stale webhook.

This can happen if any changes were made on the VCS side such as changing the default branch, renaming branches or stale webhooks. 

We recommend manually resetting webhooks as an initial troubleshooting step. If you are still having issues please submit a support ticket or reach out to support@circleci.com
